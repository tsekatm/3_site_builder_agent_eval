Who We Are

Education Changes Everything

Ubuntu Education Initiative is a Section 18A registered non-profit dedicated to improving educational outcomes in rural and township schools. We have equipped 120 schools with digital labs, trained 800 teachers in modern pedagogy, and established 45 community libraries. Our work reaches over 60,000 learners annually.

Our Programmes

What We Do

Digital Learning Labs

120 schools equipped with computer labs, tablets, and educational software. Each lab includes 30 workstations with internet connectivity and a trained facilitator.

Teacher Development

800 teachers trained in modern pedagogy, ICT integration, and inclusive teaching practices through our accredited professional development programme.

Our Impact

60,000 Learners Reached Annually

From Port Elizabeth to the rural Eastern Cape, our programmes transform schools into vibrant centres of learning. 93% of schools in our programme show improved pass rates within two years.
