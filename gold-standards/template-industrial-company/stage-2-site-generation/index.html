About TitanCore

Vanderbijlpark-based steel fabrication since 2008

TitanCore Engineering is a steel fabrication and industrial services company based in Vanderbijlpark. We specialise in structural steelwork, pressure vessels, and mining equipment fabrication.

With a 12,000m2 workshop and a team of 180 coded welders, we deliver large-scale projects on time and to specification. Our client base spans mining houses, petrochemical plants, and property developers across Sub-Saharan Africa.
