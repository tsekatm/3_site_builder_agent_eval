Our Story

Founded in 2012 by principal architect Nomsa Khumalo

Apex Architecture Studio is a multidisciplinary design practice with a portfolio spanning luxury residences, boutique hotels, and civic buildings. We blend modernist principles with African materiality.

Our projects have been featured in Architectural Digest SA and VISI Magazine. Based in Cape Town's Gardens neighbourhood, we draw inspiration from the city's unique intersection of mountain, ocean, and urban fabric.
