Who We Are

Empowering Youth Since 2014

Thrive Africa Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation focused on youth education, leadership development, and economic empowerment. Since 2014, we have awarded over 1,200 bursaries and supported 85 youth-led startups through our accelerator programme. We operate in South Africa, Mozambique, and Zambia.

Our Programmes

Our Focus Areas

Scholarships & Bursaries

Over 1,200 bursaries awarded to disadvantaged students pursuing degrees in STEM, business, and education at South African universities.

Mentorship Programmes

Pairing young professionals with industry leaders for 12-month structured mentorship in finance, technology, and public service.

Youth Startup Incubator

85 youth-led startups supported through our 6-month accelerator programme with seed funding, training, and market access.

Our Impact

Creating Lasting Change Across Southern Africa

From Johannesburg to Lusaka, our programmes have reached over 15,000 young people. 78% of our bursary graduates are employed within six months. Our startup alumni have created over 400 jobs in their communities.
