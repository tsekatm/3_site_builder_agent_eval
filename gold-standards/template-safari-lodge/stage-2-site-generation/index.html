Lodge Experiences

Immerse yourself in the raw beauty of the Karoo

Dark Sky Stargazing

Certified Dark Sky accommodation with a private observatory. Nightly guided stargazing sessions with professional telescopes and expert astronomers.

Guided Nature Walks

Explore 5,000 hectares of Karoo veld with expert guides. Discover endemic succulents, rock formations, and San rock art on trails from 2 to 12 kilometres.

Farm-to-Table Dining

Seasonal menus crafted from our kitchen garden, local farms, and Karoo lamb. Dine under the stars on our open-air terrace or in the restored farmhouse.

Off-Grid Solar Lodge

Our lodge runs entirely on solar energy and harvested rainwater. Experience luxury with zero carbon footprint in the heart of nature.

Mountain Biking

Over 40km of single-track and gravel trails across the conservancy. Bikes provided. Routes range from gentle farm roads to technical rocky descents.

Wildlife Encounters

Spot kudu, springbok, aardvark, and over 180 bird species on the conservancy. Night drives reveal bat-eared foxes and Cape porcupines.

About the Lodge

A 12-suite eco-lodge on a 5,000-hectare private conservancy

Karoo Starlight Lodge is set near Graaff-Reinet in the Eastern Cape. We are a certified Dark Sky accommodation, offering world-class stargazing alongside hiking, mountain biking, and wildlife encounters.

Our lodge runs entirely on solar energy and harvested rainwater. Each of our 12 suites features floor-to-ceiling windows framing the vast Karoo landscape, heated plunge pools, and private decks for uninterrupted stargazing.

Glimpses of life at Karoo Starlight

Karoo Night Sky

The Milky Way arches overhead in one of the darkest skies on the planet. No light pollution for hundreds of kilometres.

Sunset Landscapes

Watch the Karoo transform as the sun sets over ancient rock formations and endless open plains.

Suite Interiors

Natural stone, reclaimed timber, and handwoven textiles create warmth in our architecturally designed suites.

Karoo Cuisine

Slow-braised Karoo lamb, garden-fresh salads, and artisan breads prepared by our resident chef.

Karoo Wildlife

Springbok, kudu, and over 180 bird species roam the conservancy. Guided game walks depart at dawn.

Mountain Trails

Ancient fossil beds and San rock art await on trails through the Sneeuberg mountain range.
