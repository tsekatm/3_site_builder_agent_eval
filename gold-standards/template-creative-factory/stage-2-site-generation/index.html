About Us

Strategists, designers, and storytellers based in Johannesburg

Pixel & Grain Creative is a Johannesburg-based branding and design agency working with startups, corporates, and cultural organisations. We believe great design is a business advantage.

Our services span visual identity, packaging, digital marketing, and video production, delivered by a diverse team of strategists, designers, and storytellers from our studio at 44 Stanley in Milpark.
