Our menus change monthly, reflecting what the land offers. Each dish celebrates the freshest seasonal produce from local farms.

Local Farmers

We partner directly with smallholder farmers across the Western Cape to source organic, ethically grown ingredients.

Wine Pairings

Our sommelier curates wine pairings from Stellenbosch and Franschhoek vineyards to complement every course.

Spring Garden Salad

Heritage tomatoes, micro herbs, and edible flowers with a citrus vinaigrette.

Karoo Lamb Rack
